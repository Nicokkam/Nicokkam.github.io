About the Project

Moovyn is an indoor navigation and business intelligence app designed to enhance user experiences in large indoor spaces, such as malls, airports, museums, and hospitals. The app features interactive 3D maps, enabling users to locate points of interest, view routes, and follow step-by-step navigation to their destinations—even in foreign countries or unfamiliar layouts.

The project aimed to streamline indoor navigation while providing a valuable platform for businesses to effectively reach customers.

My role in the project was as Lead XR & Mobile Developer. I architected and implemented the software from the ground up, co-founding the company and managing the team. Although it didn’t work out on the business front, the technology successfully achieved our initial goals.

Key Features

Comprehensive Indoor Mapping

A vast collection of 3D maps for various indoor environments, eliminating the need for physical geolocation devices.

Easy Map Integration

Client-provided floorplans can be transformed into 3D models and seamlessly integrated into the app.

Business Intelligence

Collects and anonymizes user navigation and behavior data, enabling administrators to analyze visitor patterns through heat maps and other metrics. Each establishment can be weighted based on user interactions, adjusting billboard priority to enhance visibility.

Cross-Platform Mobile App Development

Designed for both Android and iOS platforms, this app incorporates the unique features of each operating system and was submited and approved for download on Google Play and the AppStore.

Marketing Opportunities:

Offers in-app advertising and targeted promotions for retailers and event organizers, with options for highlighting specific stores or services.
